SmartCost

Financial Assistant for SMEs

Overview

SmartCost is a simple financial assistant designed to help SMEs understand their costs, pricing, and profit in a practical and easy way.
Many SMEs rely on notebooks, mental calculations, and guesswork to run their finances. SmartCost provides a structured way to calculate costs, track expenses, and determine profitable selling prices.

Problem

Millions of SMEs operate daily without properly understanding:
	•	their true cost of production or service
	•	how to price their products
	•	how much profit they are actually making
This often leads to:
	•	underpricing and losses
	•	overpricing and low sales

  Solution
  
SmartCost helps SMEs:
	•	track all business costs (materials, tools, transport, labor)
	•	calculate cost per unit automatically
	•	suggest selling prices based on profit margin
	•	monitor sales and profit through a simple dashboard

  Key Features
  
	•	Production cost calculator
	•	Retail cost and pricing tracker
	•	Service-based cost estimation
	•	Weekly sales tracking (Monday–Sunday)
	•	Automatic profit and break-even calculation
	•	Simple and user-friendly interface

  Target Users
  
	•	SME owners
	•	Retail vendors
	•	Production businesses (bakers, tailors, etc.)
	•	Service providers (makeup artists, creatives, etc.)

  Impact
  
SmartCost supports the Financial Inclusion pillar, specifically financial literacy, by helping SMEs:
	•	make informed financial decisions
	•	avoid losses
	•	grow sustainably

  Scalability
  
  This solution can scale across:
•	different industries and different skill levels
•	and even different countries with similar informal economies
Future improvements include:
	•	AI-powered business insights and recommendations
	•	Monthly and yearly financial analysis
	•	User accounts and data tracking
	•	Payment and analytics integration
	•	Mobile app development
	•	Expansion to other African markets
